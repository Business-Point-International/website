What We Do

BPI empowers chosen Indian factories to develop innovative, planet and pocket friendly products.

Importers can get these products, even as a mix container, at fair pricing from acceredited factories via BPI.

“BPI is not a buying house, its a trading company. We create, we ship, we share risk and reward with you as value-adding stakeholder.”

Why choose us as your preferred supply partner?

  • Direct from Factory

    Mostly exclusive marketing tie-ups and blocked production capacities. Competitive pricing.

  • Great Products

    Future Proof. Durable. Innovative.

    Persistent R&D using AI (Artificial Intelligence) to discover and/or create unique products with professional designers and technicians. Engineered to perfection.

    Evergreen minimalist designs. Lifestyle improving functionality.
